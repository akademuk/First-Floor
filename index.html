/ABOUT OUR COMPANY

At First Floor Construction, we specialise in providing hygienic solutions for businesses where cleanliness and hygiene are of utmost importance

With years of expertise in the industry, we offer a wide range of flooring, wall, and ceiling solutions that ensure a safe and sanitary environment for your workplace. We are committed to delivering high-quality products that meet the strictest hygiene standards, making us a trusted partner for industries such as healthcare, food production, laboratories, and more. Our team not only supplies but also installs hygienic flooring, walls, and ceilings tailored to the specific needs of your business.
